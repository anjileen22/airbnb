Attractions



Check out some of these attractions that surround our Airbnb!



Queenscliffe Park


This beautiful waterfront park is just a ten minute walk away from our Airbnb. Featuring spectacular views and plenty of grassy areas with ample shade, this is the perfect place for a relaxing picnic and a spot of swimming! If you're up for some physical activity, there is also a beautiful walking trail that will lead you through native forest.



Rupa's Cafe


Pop in to Rupa's cafe for a bite of some scrumptious food. Whether you are craving bacon and eggs on toast for breakfast, tasty pies for lunch, or a sweet afternoon treat, Rupa's has a great selection of food.
